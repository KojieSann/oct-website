Bachelor of Science in
Business Administration

Special features

The Philippine Association of Colleges and Universities Commission on Accreditation (PACUCOA) has awarded this program Level II Accreditation, demonstrating our dedication to upholding strict academic standards and offering high-quality instruction. This achievement is a credit to the hard work and commitment of our esteemed professors, who offer a wealth of knowledge from backgrounds in academia and business. At Olivarez College Tagaytay, we place a strong emphasis on outcomes-based education to make sure that our students gain practical skills that are extremely relevant to their chosen fields in addition to thorough academic knowledge. With a focus on industry-related immersion programs, our curriculum is carefully crafted to be responsive to industry demands. This gives our students practical experience and facilitates a smooth transfer from the classroom to the workplace. As a result, our graduates consistently enjoy a high employability rate, reflecting the effectiveness of our holistic approach to education.

Program outcomes

With the Olivarian philosophy of education, the program believes that students should be equipped with knowledge and skills essential for successful careers in today's complex business world. A graduate of Bachelor of Science in Business Administration must be able to:

  • Analyze critically and effectively business problems utilizing fundamental business concepts and decision tools.
  • Conduct methodological research in business having familiarity with a range of data, research sources and appropriate methodologies.
  • Establish a professional presence online incorporating the key disciplines of social media, search engine optimization and business analytics.
  • Utilize information and communication technology necessary for managerial practice in business.
  • Apply knowledge of physical, social, natural sciences, and humanities in the practice of business.
  • Engage in lifelong learning with a passion to keep current with national and global developments in general, and business development in particular.
  • Perform the functional areas of business (marketing, accounting, finance, human resource management, production and operations management, information technology and strategic management) in diverse situations.
  • Practice beginning management and leadership skills using systems approach in the delivery of services.
  • Apply ethical principles in the business environment with people of diverse ethnic, cultural, gender and other backgrounds.
  • Work effectively and independently in multidisciplinary and multicultural teams.
  • Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of the ethical, legal and social obligations and responsibilities of business.
  • Exercise high moral and ethical standards in the practice of profession.
Bachelor of Science in

Business Administration

Business Administration major in Marketing Management course provides a solid foundation for a future career in marketing and business. The program helps develop the students strategic and executive competencies, prepares them to demonstrate leadership aptitudes, technological responsiveness, intellectual capacity, and decision-making skills to achieve outstanding organizational performance.

Bachelor of Science in
Accountancy

Special features

The exceptional accomplishments of Olivarez College Tagaytay are proudly shown, as demonstrated by the graduation of a Topnotcher in the May 2017 CPA Board Examination. This achievement demonstrates our dedication to academic quality, which is reinforced by a faculty roster of highly educated individuals that includes both seasoned business professionals and renowned scholars. By adopting an Outcomes-Based Education approach, we make sure that our students get practical skills that are in line with market objectives in addition to academic knowledge. Our curriculum is always adapting to new trends and international standards, making it both relevant and adaptable. Our students gain from industry-related immersion programs that close the knowledge gap between theory and practice in addition to their classroom education. Because of this, our graduates regularly have high employability rates, which is a testament to the comprehensive education they receive that fully prepares them for successful careers in their chosen sectors.

Program outcomes

With reference to the Olivarian philosophy of education, the program aims to equip students with knowledge and skills essential in competitive careers in today’s complex financial business world. A graduate of Bachelor of Science in Accountancy must be able to:

  • Develop up-to-date knowledge perspective in accounting theories and latest practice.
  • Explain and demonstrate the concept of accounting principles and higher professional integrity and demonstrate the ability in showing social responsiveness in the accounting world.
  • Demonstrate comprehensive understanding of the key aspects of the accounting principles and strategies.
  • Enhance learning experiences that prepare future financial leaders and managers for careers in all types of business and financial organizations.
  • Utilize information and communication technology necessary for accounting practice in business and industry.
  • Work effectively and independently in multidisciplinary and multicultural teams.
  • Engage in lifelong learning with a passion to keep current with national and global developments in general, and business development in particular.
  • Apply knowledge in the professional practice imbued with highest ethical standards and practice good governance and control in the financial entities.
  • Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of the ethical, legal and social obligations and responsibilities of an accountant.
  • Exercise high personal, moral and ethical standards in the practice of profession.
Bachelor of Science in

Accountancy

The Bachelor of Science in Accountancy (BSA) is a four-year degree program that prepares the students for entry level positions into a variety of fields in accountancy. These include accounting information systems, corporate accounting, and governmental accounting. It also prepares students for continuous learning through graduate programs such as the Master's Program or law.

Bachelor of Science in
Criminology

Special features

Over the years, the Olivarez College Tagaytay has continuously outperformed the country in terms of passing the Criminology Licensure Examination, which is evidence of our steadfast dedication to academic achievement. Our committed and skilled faculty members, who bring a wealth of knowledge to the classroom and guarantee that our students receive a thorough and superior education, are the reason for our success. We are proud of our strategic alliances with a number of private universities and prestigious government organizations, including the National Forensic Science Training Institute, NAPOLCOM, the Philippine National Police, the Bureau of Jail Management and Penology, and the Bureau of Fire Protection. Through these partnerships, we are able to provide our students priceless chances for practical training, industry exposure, and professional networking. Moreover, our commitment to delivering cutting-edge education is reflected in our high-end facilities, including a state-of-the-art darkroom, a polygraph machine, and advanced equipment for bullet comparison, further enhancing the practical skills and knowledge of our criminology students.

Program outcomes

In reference to the Olivarian philosophy of education, the program aims to provide quality education and training that empower students to imbibe values for national and global competitiveness. A graduate of Bachelor of Science in Criminology must be able to:

  • Conduct criminological research on crimes, crime causation, victims, and offenders to include deviant behavior;
  • Internalize the concepts of human rights and victim welfare;
  • Demonstrate competence and broad understanding in law enforcement administration, public safety and criminal justice;
  • Utilize criminalistics or forensic science in the investigation and detection of crime;
  • Apply the principles and jurisprudence of criminal law, evidence and criminal procedure;
  • Ensure offender's rehabilitation, welfare, development and reintegration to the community.
Bachelor of Science in

Criminology

The field of Criminology is the study of crime and the various agencies of justice as they operate and react to crime, criminals and victims. It is therefore the mission of the Criminology program to provide a community with professionally competent and morally upright graduates who can deliver efficient and effective services in crime prevention, crime detection and investigation, law enforcement, public safety, custody and rehabilitation of offenders, criminological research, among others.

Bachelor of Science in
Information Technology

Special features

The information technology department at Olivarez College Tagaytay is a source of pride for the college, since it is home to a group of highly skilled and knowledgeable instructors committed to educating the next generation of IT professionals. Our curriculum highlights the use of the newest programming languages and AGILE software development processes, reflecting our dedication to remaining at the forefront of technical breakthroughs. We provide students with unique growth chances through training workshops and seminars, enhancing their education with practical insights from reputable IT groups. Our Capstone Classes serve as the pinnacle of our students' academic careers, giving them a stage on which to demonstrate their expertise in software development. Additionally, our state-of-the-art facilities in programming, robotics, and multimedia contribute to creating an immersive learning environment that empowers our students to excel in the dynamic field of Information Technology.

Program outcomes

Inspired by the mission and vision of Olivarian Education, the program intends to help students in responding to the current technological concerns, thus contributing to the advancement of information technology schemes. A graduate of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology must be able to:

  • Apply knowledge of computing, science, and mathematics appropriate to the discipline;
  • Understand best practices and standards and their applications;
  • Analyze complex problems, and identify and define the computing requirements appropriate to its solution;
  • Design, implement, and evaluate computer-based systems, processes, components, or programs to meet desired needs and requirements under various constraints;
  • Effectively integrate IT-based solutions into the user environment;
  • Apply knowledge through the use of current techniques, skills, tools and practices necessary for the IT profession;
  • Understand professional, ethical, legal, security, and social issues and responsibilities in the utilization of information technology;
  • Recognize the need for and engage in planning self-learning and improving performance as a foundation for continuing professional development.
Bachelor of Science in

Information Technology

The Computer Studies Program of Olivarez College Tagaytay is an eight-semester course designed to provide the students appropriate training in the field of computing. It aims to produce graduates who are locally and globally competent in information technology. The program is composed of logical algorithms, computer concepts, and implementation of computing solutions. It develops the students' proficiency in front-end and back-end program development.

Bachelor of Science in
Hospitality Management

Special features

The Philippine Association of Colleges and Universities Commission on Accreditation (PACUCOA) awarded the Olivarez College Tagaytay Tourism Management Program Level II accreditation, demonstrating the department's unwavering dedication to innovation and excellence. We also promise to deliver an all-encompassing education by combining theory with real-world application through instructors with a wealth of academic and professional experience. We adopt state-of-the-art academic programs in order to stay responsive to global opportunities, acknowledging the dynamic nature of the hospitality and tourism industry. Our innovative, rigorous curriculum, intellectually stimulating instruction, outcome-based learning, student-industry cooperative experiences, and strong partnerships with the hospitality and tourism sector are all highlights of our student-centered approach. We also want to develop exceptional professionals with dynamic leadership and management abilities, participate in community service initiatives that are responsive, and advance knowledge by conducting significant research that is relevant to the hospitality and tourism industries. Our adherence to these values demonstrates our determination to develop the next generation of leaders and make significant contributions to the business community and wider society.

Program outcomes

In reference to the Olivarian philosophy of education, the program believes that students should be equipped with the knowledge, skills and competencies necessary for successful careers in the hospitality industry. A graduate of Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management must be able to:

  • Demonstrate knowledge of tourism industry, local tourism products and services;
  • Interpret and apply relevant laws related to tourism industry;
  • Observe and perform risk mitigation activities;
  • Utilize information technology applications for tourism and hospitality;
  • Manage and market a service-oriented business organization;
  • Demonstrate administrative and managerial skills in a service-oriented business organization;
  • Prepare and monitor industry specific financial transaction and reports;
  • Perform human capital development functions of a tourism oriented organization;
  • Utilize various communication channels proficiently in dealing with guests and colleagues;
  • Produce food products and services complying with enterprise standards;
  • Apply management skills in F & B service and operations;
  • Perform and provide full guest cycle services for front office;
  • Perform and maintain various housekeeping services for guest and facility operations;
  • Plan and implement a risk management program to provide a safe and secure workplace;
  • Provide food and beverage service and manage the operation seamlessly based on industry standards.
Bachelor of Science in

Hospitality Management

The BS Hospitality Management Program provides students with academic knowledge and a set of world-class customer service skills needed to a career in international tourism and hospitality business sectors. Having our very own Tagaytay Country Hotel, The hospitality program uses a comprehensive and work-integrated learning teaching methods in major subjects such as; Front Office Operations, Housekeeping Management, and Food and Beverage Service.

Bachelor of Science in
Nursing

Special features

The Philippine Association of Colleges and Universities Commission on Accreditation (PACUCOA) proudly awarded the Olivarez College Tagaytay Nursing Program Level II accreditation in 2016, demonstrating our school's dedication to providing top-notch instruction. With 40% of our faculty holding doctorates and 100% holding master's degrees, they are a prime example of our commitment to academic excellence. In order to enhance the educational process, we have forged partnerships that offer our students a variety of clinical experiences, guaranteeing a comprehensive education. The accomplishments of our Olivarian graduates, who have had a significant influence on society both domestically and globally, attest to the effectiveness of our strategy. Notably, from 2016 to 2018, our nursing program consistently passed the Nursing Licensure Examination with a 100% pass rate, demonstrating our unwavering commitment to producing qualified and globally competitive professionals.

Program outcomes

The college envisions becoming a dynamic and world-class health care service provider responsive to a ministry of healing and education towards transformed care providers and beneficiaries. It endeavors to provide quality health education responsive to the demands of the local, national and global communities. A graduate of Bachelor of Science in Nursing must be able to:

  • Apply knowledge of physical, social, natural and health sciences, and humanities in the practice of nursing;
  • Provide safe, appropriate and holistic care to individuals, families, population group and community utilizing nursing process;
  • Apply guidelines and principles of evidence-based practice in the delivery of care;
  • Practice nursing in accordance with existing laws, legal, ethical and moral principles;
  • Communicate effectively in speaking, writing and presenting using culturally appropriate language;
  • Prepare document that includes reporting up-to-date client care accurately and compressively;
  • Work effectively in collaboration with inters intra and multi-disciplinary and multi-cultural teams;
  • Practice beginning management and leadership skills in the delivery of client care using systems approach;
  • Conduct research with an experienced researcher;
  • Engage in lifelong learning with a passion to keep current with national and global developments in general, and nursing and health developments in particular;
  • Demonstrate responsible citizenship and pride of being a citizen of his/her own country;
  • Apply techno-intelligent care systems and processes in health care delivery;
  • Adopt the nursing core values in the practice of the profession;
  • Apply entrepreneurial skills in the delivery of nursing care.
Bachelor of Science in

Nursing

The Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) is a four-year program consisting of general education and professional courses. Professional courses begin in the first year and thread through the development of competencies up to the fourth year level. The BSN program provides an intensive nursing practicum that will refine clinical skills from the first year level to ensure basic clinical competencies required of a beginning nurse practitioner.

Bachelor of Science in
Elementary Education

Special features

An outstanding curriculum that serves as a model of excellence in teacher education is the General Education major for the Bachelor of Elementary Education at Olivarez College Tagaytay. This program trains students to become well-rounded educators by using a holistic pedagogical approach, a broad curriculum encompassing a variety of courses, considerable field immersion, and an emphasis on inclusivity and diversity. With a focus on professional growth and real-world experience, graduates perform exceptionally well on licensing exams and become vocal supporters of improvements in education. This program is dedicated to molding minds, encouraging innovation, and motivating the following generation of leaders and lifelong learners—beyond just a degree.

Program outcomes

Animated by the Olivarian philosophy of education, the program commits to inculcate a passion for excellence. As a catalyst for change, it instills concern for quality education geared towards national and global competitiveness. A graduate of Bachelor in Elementary Education must be able to:

  • Demonstrate in-depth understanding of the diversity of learners in various learning area;
  • Manifest meaningful and comprehensive pedagogical content knowledge (PCK) of the different subject areas;
  • Utilize appropriate assessment and evaluation tools to measure learning outcomes;
  • Manifest skills in communication, higher order thinking and use of tools and technology to accelerate learning and teaching;
  • Demonstrate positive attributes of a model teacher, both as an individual and as a professional;
  • Manifest a desire to continuously pursue personal and professional development.
Bachelor of Science in

Elementary Education

The BEED is an undergraduate teacher education degree program designed to prepare individuals intending to teach at the elementary level. The BEED Degree program aims to develop highly motivated and competent teachers specializing in the content and pedagogy of elementary education. After completing all academic requirements of the degree, graduates of BEED should be able to practice the teaching profession at the elementary level.
