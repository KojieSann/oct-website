ABM

Accountancy, Business and Management

ABM is a str and that focuses on the basic concepts of financial management, business management, and corporate operations. Students will engage in business simulation and be exposed to the types of decisions business managers face regularly. ABM also helps students improve their skills, management strategies, and knowledge of managing a business and becoming a successful manager and accountant someday.

HUMSS

Humanities and Social Sciences

Humanities are branches of knowledge that have an interest in the human person and their cultures for him to be developed holistically, leading to an appreciation of human values and the unique ability of the human spirit to articulate itself. Humanities uses philosophy, language, religion, literature, history, art, and music to be equipped for 21st-century skills. The branches of knowledge under Humanities prepare learners to pursue higher education or acquire middle-level skills, or geared towards employment or entrepreneurship with humane involvement with the sense of connectivity to facticity and the existentialism of our fallenness.

STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics

STEM is an educational program that prepares secondary students for college in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. This track focuses on advanced concepts and topics that emphasize applying knowledge to real-life situations. The academic concepts are coupled with a variety of exciting activities and project-based learning under a curriculum of an interdisciplinary approach. Learners' multiple intelligences are well-catered and accustomed to improving the school, community, and global interest.

GAS

General Academics Strand

GAS is excellent for students who must decide which track to take. It is just a guide point in the students' lives to help them decide what course or degree they want to pursue in college.
A student can choose electives from the different academic strands under this track. These subjects include Humanities, Social Sciences, Applied Economics, Organization and Management, Disaster Preparedness, and Electives that will allow students the flexibility to take courses aligned with their passion and interest.

TVL / HE

Home Economics

TVL Strand is designed to develop students' skills that are useful for livelihood and technical projects. It provides a curriculum combining Core Courses and specialized hands-on courses that meet the competency-based assessment of TESDA. At the end of the course, students will be able to take the TESDA National Certification Assessments. In Olivarez College Tagaytay, the students can select one of the two unique courses, Home Economics and Information and Communication Technology.
The TVL-HE Strand offers three specializations namely, Bread and Pastry, Food and Beverage, and Cookery.

TVL / ICT

Information and Communication Technology

TVL Strand is designed to develop students' skills that are useful for livelihood and technical projects. It provides a curriculum combining Core Courses and specialized hands-on courses that meet the competency-based assessment of TESDA. At the end of the course, students will be able to take the TESDA National Certification Assessments. In Olivarez College Tagaytay, the students can select one of the two unique courses, Home Economics and Information and Communication Technology.
In the TVL-ICT Strand, the students will be taking subjects that will develop ICT Skills, which will significantly help technology-based jobs. After completing the course, the students can take the Computer System Servicing Assessment from TESDA and continue higher degree courses in ICT or Computer Science.
