OCT HISTORY

Olivarez College Tagaytay is accredited by the Philippine Association of Colleges and Universities Commission on Accreditation (PACUCOA). The college is duty recognized by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) and the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA).

Olivarez College Tagaytay is a private, non-sectarian educational institution conceived and established in the belief that providing educational opportunities for the masses is the best legacy one can offer to the Filipino nation and the world.
Located in the sprawling and picturesque Olivarez Plaza, Olivarez College Tagaytay was established in 2003 with the vision of bringing quality tertiary education to the youth of Cavite and nearby provinces. To achieve this vision, the college has been sensitive to the ever-changing demands of the information driven world by constantly re-engineering its curricular offerings. The commitment of the College to its students does not end after graduation. The placement services of the College include an extensive industry network system, coupled with very positive industry network system and very positive community image. Thus graduates are employed in prestigious companies, hospitals and schools.

Our Core
Values

The academic capacity and vitality of Olivarez College flow from, and are nourished by the core values which underpin the Strategic Plan, and are reflected in all Olivarez College activities.

Honesty

Honesty and high ethical standards will inform all actions of Olivarez College as an academic enterprise dedicated to scholarship, discovery and creativity. These standards are the hallmark of our success and our excellence as a global university.

Excellence

Olivarez College is committed to building excellence throughout its educational, research and outreach programs, involving every unit of the institution and every member of the Olivarez College community. This pursuit of excellence is driven by our respect for knowledge, our passion for learning, and our concern for human well-being.

Accountability

A sustained commitment to openness and accountability is critical to Olivarez Collegeâ€™s success as a public institution of international standing. The best interests of Olivarez College are served by having in place structures which ensure accountability at all levels and a commitment to raising quality through continuous improvement.

Responsible Enquiry

The freedom and responsibility of all stakeholders to engage in critical enquiry and intellectual discourse is vital to the advancement of the frontiers of knowledge and the pursuit of higher truths. Within the Olivarez College community, academic faculty should take the lead in promoting the rational debate of ideas and constructive problem-solving based on academic values and priorities.

Teamwork

We seek to create interdisciplinary, synergistic and collegial relationships characterized by honesty, collaboration, inclusiveness and flexibility. Fostering civil dialogue, promoting collegial exchanges and cultivating mutual respect are essential to our healthy development as an increasingly diverse and robust community of students, alumni, faculty and staff. These values support a robust academic culture which nurtures our academic enterprise and intellectual life.

Educating the
MIND, BODY AND SOUL

"Educating the MIND, BODY AND SOUL" goes the Olivarian Motto. These words capsulate the Olivarian Philosophy of Education committed to the development of the critical mind capable of intelligent decision-making and analysis; physically fit body that is well versed with arts, skill and athletics. A sound mind make up a total person with a spirit that is in communion with Christian ideals, and one who is dedicated in the service of God, country and men.

Mission

Olivarez College Tagaytay is committed to provide high quality education, cultivate values and nurture a culture of excellence underscoring individual and social advancement.

Vision

Olivarez College Tagaytay is a leading global 21st-century educational institution known for holistic transformative education and excellent research outcomes geared towards local and national development.

Philosophy of Olivarian Education
Olivarian education endeavors to combine the best in human and Christian tradition with awareness of, and involvement in the present day demands of national and global development. The earnest striving for self-realization is, therefore, directed to find its fulfillment in responsible services that will significantly contribute to the common effort towards national and global development.
