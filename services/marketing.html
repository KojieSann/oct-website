Marketing and Admission Office

Olivarez College Tagaytay's Marketing and Admissions Office is the nerve center of the institution's enrollment strategies. Their main responsibility is to create and implement extensive marketing campaigns. The office leads the recruitment drives and actively involves prospective students in the admissions process.

List of Admission Requirements

New Students (SHS)

  • Certificate of Good Moral (original)
  • F137
  • F138 (Original)
  • PSA (photo copy)
  • Passport size picture (2pcs)

For Transferees (SHS)

  • Certificate of Good Moral (original)
  • F137 (recent)
  • F138 (certified true copy)
  • PSA (photo copy)
  • Passport size picture (2pcs)

For College Freshmen

  • Certificate of Good Moral (original)
  • F137 (shs)
  • PSA (photo copy)
  • Passport size picture (2pcs)

For College Irregular Students/Transferee

  • Certificate of Good Moral (original)
  • Transfer Credential (TOR for Evaluation and TOR copy for OCT)
  • F137 (SHS) photocopy
  • PSA (photo copy)
  • Passport size picture (2pcs)

For Foreign Students

  • Certificate of Alien Eligibility
  • Photocopy of Passport and Visa Validity
  • Recent Medical ( 1 month upon arrival)
  • NBI Clearance
  • Certificate of Quarantine from accredited hospital or clinic
  • Certificate of grades from last school attended from the origin country
  • Picture 1x1 (4pcs)
  • Picture 2x2( 2pcs)
