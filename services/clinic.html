SCHOOL CLINIC

In cooperation with students and other student-centered offices of the University, the Olivarez College Tagaytay School Clinic aims to be a compassionate, service-oriented facility that supports the best possible health and well-being of the Olivarian community by offering high-quality, easily accessible primary outpatient medical services as well as pertinent health programs.

    The school clinic provides the following services:

  • Acute care services include triaging and referrals, medical certificate/clearance issuance, medical consultation and evaluation, and first aid.
  • Immunization service (administration of certain vaccines, review of immunization history, and advice on necessary immunizations)
  • A thorough evaluation of one's health (student health record, health screening for internships, clearances for fieldwork at different year levels).
