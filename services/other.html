ACCOUNTING OFFICE

The Olivarez College Tagaytay's accounting office is in charge of providing financial information, keeping track of financial transactions, supervising and monitoring revenue, and overseeing expenditures.

The Accounting Office is also in charge of overseeing the University's cash flow to make sure there are sufficient resources available to cover ongoing expenses. The office's eight divisions offer a wide range of services, including:

    The Accounting Office provides the following services:
  • Section Payroll
  • Section of Accounts Payable
  • Section of Accounts Receivable
  • Section of Property Accounting
  • Section of Bookkeeping
  • The Payment Section
  • Section of Cashiers
SCHOOL CANTEEN

Educational resources such as school canteens and other food services are crucial. In addition to being an essential component of the school environment, they play a significant role in providing food for students and the school community.

The school canteen should support and enhance students' learning while reflecting the institution's educational objectives. When eaten regularly, the food served in the school canteen may make up to one-third of a student's daily caloric intake, substantially impacting their nutrition and general health.

BOOKSTORE

Books, souvenirs, and OCT apparel can all be purchased at the Olivarez College Tagaytay Bookstore. It is the central location for lab coats, PE and NSTP shirts, and textbooks. It provides a handy location where offices and students can buy regularly used office and school supplies. Additionally, it provides photocopying and printing services in-store. Additionally, the OCT Bookstore offers:

  • Additional required or recommended reading for the pupils
  • Office and school supplies, mainly for Olivarez College Tagaytay staff and students
