CENTER FOR RESEARCH PUBLICATION PLANNING AND DEVELOPMENT (CRPPD)

The Office of the Vice President for Academics and Services, through the Center for Research, Publication, Planning, and Development (CRPPD) aims to cultivate a research culture and enhance its stakeholders' research capabilities, including Administrators, Faculty, Students, and Academic Support Staff. To achieve this, the center regularly organizes in-house seminar workshops and supports external seminars, training, and conferences on research methodology, statistics, ethical considerations, presentation, and publication of research articles.

Moreover, the establishment of the Center for Research Planning and Development (CRPPD), led by the College Dean, is a significant step in creating a research-oriented environment at our institution. The CRPPD plays a vital role in promoting active participation in research activities among Olivarians through its comprehensive approach that covers all aspects of research, known as the 5 Ps: Promotion, Production, Presentation, Publication, and Practice.

The institution's core values of Honesty, Excellence, Accountability, Responsible Inquiry, and Teamwork (HEART) guide us towards a higher purpose over the next five years. The Olivarez College Tagaytay envisions this path as a smooth trajectory that will positively change the lives we impact. Our commitment to research, education, and core values underscores our collective effort.
